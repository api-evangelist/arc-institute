BCD Travel uses the serious game Pacific to provide its employees across the globe with leadership training
The company saved up to 60% thanks to this training program – BCD Travel is a company specialized in corporate travel management. It was created to provide companies with a quality service to keep their employees safe and productive during business trips. With 13,500 employees in 109 countries, the company’s 2017 turnover topped $25,000 million.
